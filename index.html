Baking

Delicious No-Kneed Baguettes

Make three fluffy and delicious baguettes, using a simple
folding technique, instead of labor-intensive kneading.

  • ACTIVE TIME
    40 MINS
  • TOTAL TIME
    3 HRS
  • CALORIES
    1670
  • SKILL LEVEL
    MEDIUM

INGREDIENTS

grams
lbs, oz
  • 500g bread flour
  • 400g cool water
  • 10g salt
  • 7.5g yeast
  • Extra flour(for dusting)

EQUIPMENT

  • Large Bowl
  • Plastic wrap
  • Baking sheet or stone
  • Bench scraper
  • Thermometer
